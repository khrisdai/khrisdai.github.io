Project S@ndwich

We recently conducted a survey targeting first-generation American immigrants, aiming to gain insight into their parents' healthcare needs and identify any gaps.

Survey Population

Over 75% of the people surveyed say their parents stay in the U.S. for over 3 months. Most are visiting on a B2 visitor visa.

The age of their parents range from 55-80, with an average age of 65.

85% of them have one or more chronic conditions such as hypertension, diabetes.

2/3 of them take prescription drugs.

Length of Stay
Survey Results

60% purchased travel insurance or short-term health insurance.

The rest 40% say they did not purchase insurance because

  • didn't find existing products valuable (e.g., too expensive and too restrictive)
  • didn't think it would be needed
  • not sure what to buy

最近，我们进行了一项针对第一代美国移民的问卷调查，旨在深入了解他们父母的医疗需求以及目前存在的不足之处。

调查对象

大部分受访者的父母持B2探亲签证来访美国，超过75%在美国逗留长达3个月以上。

父母的年龄在55至80岁之间，平均年龄为65岁。

85%患有一种或多种慢性病，比如高血压、糖尿病等。

有三分之二的人需要服用处方药。

逗留时间
调查结果

大家都知道美国医疗费用为世界之最。为了避免经济上负担，60%的人选择购买旅行保险。 其余40%选择裸奔的朋友们表示他们没有购买保险，原因主要有：

  • 现有的产品价值不高（例如，价格太贵、限制太多）
  • 觉得不会用到
  • 不确定应该购买什么
