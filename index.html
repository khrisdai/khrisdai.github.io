Project Tetromino

The financing of U.S. heathcare is messed up, with numerous insurance plans, each with different networks, costs, and coverage. This complexity often leads to decision fatigue and suboptimal choices.

Project Tetromino elevates your insurance literacy by sharing insider knowledge, so you can avoid the hidden traps.

Project S@ndwich

In a "4-2-1" family structure, there are four grandparents, two parents, and one child. Often referred to as the Sandwich Generation, the middle-tier adults face significant burden to support multiple generations financially and emotionally.

Project S@ndwich explores what can be done to support individuals in this modern family structure.

Project MBTI

Can we curate a set of personas, akin to the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) tailored specifically for health insurance, based on individuals' healthcare needs, preferences, and behaviors?

Project MBTI aims to simplify the decision-making process to empower you to make informed choices for your health coverage.
